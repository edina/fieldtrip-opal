The Tree Health Survey has three set steps

  1. Download and print the survey documents in English or in Welsh (ideally in colour).
  2. Find a site with safe access to one or more broadleaved trees and start surveying!
  3. Submit result via their simple online form.

There are two main areas of interest here which can be broke into two distinct area: (a) The survey and the questions which are contained within it, and (b) the educational/support documentation contained within the resource.
